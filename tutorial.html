How to make a Pizza

  1. Step 1

    Use eggs and flour to make the dough

  2. Step 2

    Knead the dough and let it rise. Roll out to make your pizza base

  3. Step 3

    Make the tomato and spread over the pizza base

  4. Step 4

    Add all the veg and meat to your pizza. Add your favourite toppings!

  5. Step 5

    Cook the pizza and slice using a pizza slicer

  6. Step 6

    EAT!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!
